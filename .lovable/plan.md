

# Personal Portfolio & Resume Website

A minimal and clean personal website for a software/tech professional, combining portfolio showcase with resume functionality.

## Pages & Sections

### 1. Home / Hero
- Clean hero section with your name, title (e.g., "Software Engineer"), and a brief tagline
- Professional photo/avatar placeholder
- Quick links to your social profiles (GitHub, LinkedIn, etc.)
- Smooth scroll navigation to sections below

### 2. About
- Short bio paragraph about who you are and what you do
- Key skills displayed as clean tags/badges
- Years of experience, location, and availability status

### 3. Projects / Portfolio
- Grid of project cards with thumbnail, title, description, and tech stack tags
- Links to live demo and source code for each project
- Filter by technology or category

### 4. Experience / Resume
- Timeline-style work experience section
- Education history
- Downloadable resume button (PDF link)

### 5. Contact
- Simple contact form (name, email, message)
- Email and social media links
- Call-to-action to get in touch

## Design Details
- Light theme with lots of whitespace and clean typography
- Subtle hover animations on cards and links
- Fully responsive (mobile-first)
- Sticky navigation header with smooth scrolling
- Consistent spacing and minimal color palette (monochrome with one accent color)
